Master Thesis on Extremal Combinatorics

Master thesis in Extremal graph theory using Finite geometry.

Read the Master Thesis ->

Abstract

We analyse the constructions of extremal bipartite free graphs using finite geometry. We begin exploring the relevant theoretical background, context, and early development in the case t = 3. The main focus is to provide a clear and thorough exposition of the proof of Kt+1,(t−1)!−1-freeness of the norm graph given by Ball and Pepe BP16. The reader without the relevant knowledge of the fields of algebraic geometry, conmutative algebra and finite geometry can obtain an good understanding of the work presented in BP16.
